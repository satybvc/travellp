Privacy Policy

At TravelsNest.com, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when using our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you access our website or use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

1. Personal Information

This includes details you provide when making a booking or creating an account, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing address
  • Payment details

2. Travel Preferences

To enhance your travel experience, we may collect information about:

  • Preferred destinations
  • Travel dates
  • Seating choices
  • Frequent flyer details

3. Device & Usage Information

We collect technical data about how you access our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Website interaction and browsing behavior

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience
  • Personalize content and advertisements
  • Analyze website traffic and improve functionality
  • How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for purposes such as:

  • Processing Bookings & Payments - Handling reservations, billing, and transactions.
  • Customer Support - Responding to inquiries, requests, and feedback.
  • Personalized Experience - Tailoring services based on your preferences.
  • Website & Service Improvement - Enhancing performance and functionality.
  • Communications & Updates - Sending booking confirmations, promotions, and other relevant travel details.
  • Legal Compliance - Adhering to legal requirements and enforcing our Terms of Service.

Data Protection & Security

We implement security measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of your personal data.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers (for booking and payment processing)
  • Legal Authorities (when required by law)
  • Marketing Partners (only with your consent)

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request data deletion (where applicable)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@travelsnest.com.
